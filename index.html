Artists mini bio

Andrés Isaza-Giraldo [ download CV ] Media artist born in Manizales, Colombia. He studied Filmmaking at the National University of Colombia and Multimedia Art at the University of Lisbon. He has directed the short-films Medias blancas (2017), Sereno (2022) and The Last Film on Earth (2023), alongside several music videos, video-essays and interactive experiences. He has an interest for video post-production and machine learning technologies. His work explores the relationship of time in life, history and cinema.
